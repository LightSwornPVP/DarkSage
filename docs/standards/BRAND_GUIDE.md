# DarkSage Brand & Documentation Guide
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Brand Principle
**Wisdom Over Noise**

DarkSage should feel intelligent, restrained, trustworthy, and deliberate.

## Documentation Palette
For digital/print design:
- Obsidian: `#111318`
- Slate: `#2A2F3A`
- Parchment: `#F4F1E8`
- Sage Gold: `#B79A57`
- Signal Green: `#2E8B57`
- Warning Amber: `#C58A1B`
- Risk Red: `#A33A3A`

Colors are semantic aids, never the sole carrier of meaning.

## Logo
Until an approved logo asset exists, use the wordmark **DarkSage**. Do not invent or substitute an unofficial logo.

## Covers
Use restrained typography, generous whitespace, document ID, title, version, status, owner, classification, and motto.

## Accessibility
Maintain readable contrast, never encode status by color alone, and provide text labels for icons.
